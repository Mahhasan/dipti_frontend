Join Our Franchise

Daffodil International Professional Training Institute (a concern of Daffodil Computers Ltd.) or DIPTI is a country renowned professional training providing institute. DIPTI was established on June, 2004 with a vision to provide a very wide range of job oriented and professional development training to convert both semiskilled and unskilled people to human resource. DIPTI is also an affiliated institute of Bangladesh Technical Education Board. Beside this DIPTI has links and partnership with University and Industry Alliance, University of Dhaka, IIC Malaysia and CEA UK.

To make the vision success DIPTI welcomes entrepreneurs to be a proud partner for doing social business so that training can be reached to the doorsteps of the trainees through them. DIPTI developed a very dedicated and expert team to provide support to the partners even from inception of this education/training business to ensure profit with social status. If you are confident enough and decided to start a social business or if you want to shape your existing business with branding image you are requested to contact with us following the procedure mentioned bellow:

Read the defined rules and resolution to be a partner
